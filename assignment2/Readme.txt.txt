JAVASCRIPT VALIDATION
- Recommendation: filters restaurants by dietary preference, budget, and purpose
- Registration: validates username, email, phone, password, gender, country
- Reservation: validates all fields, blocks past dates, shows/hides payment fields
- Bill calculator: multiplies group size by average cost per person dynamically

KNOWN ISSUES
- All deposits are $0 as real restaurants do not require one

About By the Moon

By the Moon is a restaurant discovery and reservation platform built around the Glenferrie Road dining strip in Hawthorn, Melbourne. It was designed with the local community in mind — students, residents, and anyone passing through who wants to know where to eat without the guesswork.

We bring together a curated selection of restaurants spanning Japanese, Korean, Italian, Malaysian, Middle Eastern, and Vietnamese cuisines, all within easy reach of Swinburne University of Technology. Browse menus, get a personalised recommendation, and lock in your table — all in one place.
